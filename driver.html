Reach the lava boi to beat the maze.

Avoid lava and finish in under 300 moves.

CONTROLS:
  • W moves camera forward.
  • A moves camera to the left
  • S moves camera backwards
  • D moves camera to the right.
  • P is for cheaters. Use it to see the maze.
  • J is another cheater button. Use it for infinite jumps!
  • Camera can be rotated with the arrow keys.
